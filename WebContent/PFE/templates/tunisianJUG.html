Esprit Tunisian JUG

JUG stands for Java ™ User Group is an independent, international, non-profit organization that brings together stakeholders and actors who share an interest in the Java ™ technology. ESPRIT JUG is the first JUG organization active in Tunisia. We are a group of teachers scientists, engineers, professionals and students who aims to promote the Java ™ technology through conferences regularly. ESPRIT JUG hopes to play an important role in promoting the Java ™ technology spikes in Tunisia.
